About Me

As an Eagle Scout, I have always been driven by a commitment to serve and help others. This dedication led me to work as a first responder from the age of 18 to 21, where I gained invaluable experience and honed my problem-solving skills as an EMT-Basic. However, my true passion lies in technology, where I see endless possibilities to positively impact lives. Transitioning into the tech field, I am currently a computer science student at the University of North Texas, where I strive to blend my background in emergency response with innovative tech solutions.


Throughout my academic journey, I have achieved notable milestones that underscore my technical proficiency and creative problem-solving abilities. I ranked second place at the 2023 NSBE Hackathon at the University of North Texas, showcasing my ability to collaborate and innovate under pressure. Additionally, I am CompTIA A+ certified and have earned a FreeCodeCamp certification in Responsive Web Design. My goal is to leverage my skills to develop applications that simplify and enhance everyday life, continuing my commitment to helping others through technology.
